About Us

AC/DC Productions is a revolutionary theater company that brings interactive storytelling to life. Unlike traditional plays, we allow the audience to decide what happens next. Each show is a unique experience where viewers shape the story in real time.

Our mission is to engage, surprise, and immerse audiences by making them part of the performance. Whether it's a thrilling murder mystery, an exciting kids' adventure, or a futuristic sci-fi experience, every show is dynamic and full of unexpected twists!
